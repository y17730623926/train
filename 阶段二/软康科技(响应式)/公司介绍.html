01

COMPANY PROFILE

公司概况

1988年进入房地产行业，经过三十余年的发展，成为国内领先的房地产公司，目前主营业务包括房地产开发和物业服务。公司聚焦城市圈带的发展战略，截至2015年底，公司进入中国大陆66个城市，分布在以珠三角为核心的广深区域、以长三角为核心的上海区域、以环渤海为核心的北京区域，以及由中西部中心城市组成的成都区域。此外，公司自2013年起开始尝试海外投资，目前已经进入香港、新加坡、旧金山、纽约、伦敦等5个海外城市。 Enter the real estate industry in 1988, after more than 30 years of development, become a leading real estate company, the current main business including real estate development and property services. Company focused on the development strategy of the city ring, by the end of 2015, the company entered the Chinese mainland in 66 cities, distribution in the pearl river delta as the core of the guangzhou region, the Yangtze river delta as the core of Shanghai area, bohai sea as the core area of Beijing, and from the Midwest regional central city of chengdu. In addition, the company since 2013 and started trying to overseas investment, at present already in Hong Kong, Singapore, 5 overseas cities such as San Francisco, New York and London.
